Dyslexia Facts

1.

Dyslexia is a specific learning disability that affects reading, spelling, and writing abilities.

2.

People with dyslexia may have trouble with word recognition and decoding, making reading fluently a challenge.

3.

Dyslexia is estimated to affect about 5-10% of the population.

4.

Dyslexia is characterized by difficulties with phonological processing, which is the ability to recognize and manipulate sounds in words.

5.

Dyslexia is not related to vision problems, but rather a difference in the way the brain processes language.
